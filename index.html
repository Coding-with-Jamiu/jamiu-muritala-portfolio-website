Brief About Me

I'm JAMIU MURITALA, a proud graduate of Sociology from the prestigious University of Ibadan. My passion lies in the world of design, encompassing everything from graphic design to web and mobile app development. With a keen eye for aesthetics and a dedication to creating visually compelling and user-friendly designs, I am committed to transforming ideas into engaging digital experiences. My background in Sociology provides me with a unique perspective on user behavior and interaction, which I leverage to enhance the usability and appeal of my designs. With my extensive knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, CorelDraw, Photoshop, and more, I can design anything you need. I have experience working full-time, remotely, in hybrid roles, and as a consultant, making me adaptable to any position you require. I am also open to relocating and working anywhere in the world that offers opportunities for growth and learning.
